Use this service to find your NHS number.

An NHS Number is a unique 10 digit number used to identify you and match you to your health records. Everyone registered with the NHS in England, Wales and the Isle of Man has their own number.

Before you start

You must have an email address or phone number registered with an NHS service to continue online.

To continue you will need:

  • access to your email or mobile phone
Start
Other ways to find your NHS number

You can find your NHS number on any document sent to you by the NHS. This may include:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral letters
  • appointment letters

You can also ask your GP practice for help if you can't find your NHS number.
