Find your NHS Number

{{content_title}}

For security reasons we must verify who you are before we can give you your NHS number. We will do this by searching for your contact details in our database and then sending you a security code.

  1. Enter your details

    Tell us your name, gender, date of birth and postcode. We can then find your contact information in our patient database.

  2. Get a security code

    Using the contact details we hold in our database, we will send you a one-time verification code.

  3. Receive your NHS number

    When your code is verified, you will then be shown your NHS number.

By submitting your details in this service you agree to let us search for your details in our database to send you a security code.
